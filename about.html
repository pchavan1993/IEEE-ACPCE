ABOUT IEEE

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) ,founded in the year 1884, is an association dedicated to advancing innovation and technological excellence. With its base spread across 160 countries around the world, it is the worlds largest technical professional society.
The organisation has a membership count of over 400,000 and with 2200 student chapters in various colleges and universities, its sole purpose is nurturing the interest of innovation into the minds of hundreds of students across the world. It has the distinction of publishing more than 30% of all technological papers.  
It aims to provide current and crucial information to professionals around the globe, which in turn propels them to the top in their independent feilds.  
For more information visit: www.ieee.org  

ABOUT IEEE-ACPCE

The ACPCE Student Chapter of IEEE is a registered establishment which aims at progressing with a determined mindset towards gathering the students of ACPCE to participate in various events and workshops, helping in the overall growth and knowledge of the students.The goal of IEEE ACPCE is inculcating interest among students with regards to research as well as application of the knowledge that we receive in our engineering education.

WHAT IEEE OFFERS YOU ??

IEEE-TV

 

IEEE.tv produces special-interest programming for the benefit of IEEE's members. IEEE.tv is an award winning, internet based television network, made possible by the members of IEEE. The IEEE.tv website also provides many features free of charge to the general public. New programs, recorded in high definition, are added monthly.

 

IEEE-EXPLORE

 

IEEE Xplore provides Web access to almost 3-million technical and scientific documents.The content in IEEE Xplore comprises 151 journals, over 900 conference proceedings, more than 3,400 technical standards, over 400 ebooks and 200 educational courses. Approximately 25,000 new documents are added to IEEE Xplore each month.

 

IEEE-MEMBERSHIP

Through IEEE, you can find colleagues who share your vision - who are moving technology forward today. As a member, you'll be presented with new resources, valuable opportunities and many discounts that will help you advance your career in the right direction. Memberships build a platform to introduce technology careers to students around the world.
